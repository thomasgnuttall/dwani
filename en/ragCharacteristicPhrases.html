Carnatic Melody Exploration

Besides a particular set of svaras and the emphasis given to some of them, the personality of a raga is also determined by the melodic movement between its svaras. Some of these movemes are frequently used, and become defining traits of the raga. This tool helps you to visualize this movement across svaras, and to pay attention to the characteristic melodic phrases of the performed raga. Read the instructions below.
